Good customer service starts with contact. The easier you make that contact, the happier your clients will be. Kenmore Design’s easy to use live chat system enables customers to reach you easily and instantly during the hours of your choosing, via your website or Trader’s Room portal.

The system provides the ability for you to create and manage live chat operators, chat departments, and languages. For better service, live chat operators will have the ability to view clients’ geographical location.

The live chat system will be deeply integrated into your Trader’s Room or website, giving your traders the confidence that there is always someone to talk to.

Live chat is included at no additional cost with your Trader’s Room, and is also available as a standalone service.

The Kenmore Design live chat system offers the following functionality:

For your traders: Initiate chats and select departments.

For your operators: Chat and transfer chats between agents, manage canned responses, and get desktop notifications.

For Trader’s Room administrators: Manage operators, departments, chat languages, and workflows

In addition, our live chat system has over 100 other features.

All Kenmore Design products can be offered in multiple languages, from your website to the Trader’s Room and CRM.

Using our multiple language plug-ins allows you populate your website in an unlimited number of languages. Special geo-location logic can be deployed to detect each visitor’s region and serve the website in the local language.

No need to rush: we know it takes a long time to perfect the website copy, and it can be especially time-consuming in a foreign language. Once deployed, the plug-in can enable and disable certain languages; this way, you can launch the language when you think the copy is ready.

Trader’s Room allows language switching similar to that of the website. Once language preference is set by the user (or auto-detected by the geo-location logic), the user’s confirmation emails are optimized for that language as well.

Support-related issues account for a lot of forex brokerage traffic. Kenmore Design offers a support ticket system to help reduce this traffic. Time spent forwarding emails is reduced, as issues will be directed to the appropriate agent at once via the tickets, which are created by your clients and assigned to the agents inside your office based on topic, department, or expertise

The support ticket system is extremely easy to use. Agents can respond to issues right within the system, reassign tickets, make internal notes, manage attachments, and more

The system is equipped with notification reminders, so no ticket will ever get lost. Multiple reminders are sent to ensure that each issue is attended to on time.
